Guide to Use Trojan GYM Management System

Explore the features and navigate through our website with ease.

1. Home Page

After logging in, you will be redirected to the home page. Here, you can find a search bar to quickly look up categories, and highlights of our website features. Explore package categories or learn more about our offerings by navigating through the top menu.

2. Packages and Categories

In the Packages section, view the various GYM packages we offer. Click "More Details" to learn about specific packages.
In the Categories section, browse the available workout categories such as Yoga, Cardio, and Zumba. Clicking "More Details" provides comprehensive information about each category.

3. About Us

Learn more about our GYM, mission, and values on the About Us page. Explore the profiles of our experienced trainers, and click "Get Info" to view detailed information about each trainer.

4. Booking

Enroll in your desired categories and packages through the Booking section. Follow these steps:

  1. Select a category of interest.
  2. Choose a package (with or without a trainer).
  3. If selecting a trainer, pick your preferred trainer.
  4. Choose the package type and a time slot.
  5. Proceed to the confirmation page, complete payment, and receive an invoice.
Your booking will be saved in your profile for future reference.

5. Profile Management

Manage your bookings and personal details in your profile. Features include:

  • View and download your booking invoices.
  • Cancel bookings (within 12 hours of booking).
  • Update your profile picture and username.
  • Manage your password and set a security key for recovery purposes.
Remember: Your security key is crucial for recovering your password. Contact admin support if needed.

6. Footer Features

At the bottom of the website, you will find the following:

  • Contact Us: Reach out to the admin directly for questions or support.
  • Special Events: Learn about the exciting events happening at our GYM.
  • FAQ: Find answers to common questions.
  • Social Media: Stay connected with us through our social media channels.
